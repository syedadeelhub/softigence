ABOUT
Softigence is a global management consulting and solution implementation consultancy firm. Our focus is on working closely with the SAS Institute to leverage their leading enterprise solution and analytics platform, providing organizations with a comprehensive 'Enterprise Solution.'

Softigence is proud to be a part of the SAS Partner Program, offering established expertise in various risk solutions to deliver the best possible business and analytical solutions to the industry. Our strategy at Softigence is aligned with SAS's strategy, ensuring that our team is fully equipped and up to date with the latest innovative solutions.

GRC CONSULTING

The GRC landscape is ever-changing. We have implemented multiple layers of GRC for our customers, including Operational Risk, Compliance Risk, Information Security Risk, Business Continuity, Internal Audit, and Risk Appetite Scoring, among others.
